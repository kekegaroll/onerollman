Banishing Crystal
Quest Item
Unique
Use: Summons a banishing portal, which costs an apexis shard to power. Can only be used at Forge Camp: Wrath and Forge Camp: Terror and only works on the demons therein. The portal lasts 3 min and has an effective range of 30 yards. (1 minute cooldown)
