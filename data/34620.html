King Mrgl-Mrgl's Spare Suit
Quest Item
Unique
Use: Wear King Mrgl-Mrgl's spare murloc suit. It can only be used at Winterfin Village and within the Winterfin Caverns. Will only fool the aggressive Winterfin murlocs. Many actions, including combat and casting beneficial spells, will drop the disguise.
